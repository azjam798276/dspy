# Strategic Engineering Patterns

## Alignment
1. **Business Value:** Every technical decision must trace back to a business KPI.
2. **Resource Efficiency:** Optimize for developer productivity and long-term maintenance costs.
3. **Risk Management:** Proactively identify and mitigate single points of failure.

## Culture & Process
- Foster psychological safety in code reviews.
- Automate repetitive tasks to reduce toil.

## Optimization Strategy
- Refined based on feedback: Focus on high-level system health and team velocity.
